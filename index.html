Our Partners

Darsh B Jain
Chartered Accountant

Darsh B is a Chartered Accountant with over 6 years of experience in taxation, business advisory, and financial compliance. Operating out of the Bangalore office, he has served clients across industries—from startups to mid-sized enterprises—by helping them streamline operations, maintain compliance, and drive financial sustainability. His focus includes bookkeeping, GST, TDS, and payroll compliance, as well as MIS and budget preparation.

He has been instrumental in designing and implementing SOPs that enhance internal controls and reporting efficiencies. Darsh has successfully led management consultancy projects in sectors such as retail, logistics, F&B, and professional services, offering practical solutions tailored to the specific needs of each organization. His involvement ensures a value-driven approach to client service.

In addition, Darsh contributes to strategic initiatives such as financial due diligence, working capital optimization, and automation of financial reporting. His expertise is reflected in his ability to structure business processes, develop monthly management review frameworks, and support investor reporting through accurate reconciliations and analysis. His hands-on involvement in client delivery ensures exceptional service quality and long-term impact.

Jithesh S
Chartered Accountant

Jithesh S is a Chartered Accountant with a strong foundation in audit and accounting services. He completed his articleship with MSKA & Associates (Associated with BDO) in Bangalore, specializing in accounting, audit and financial reporting. After qualifying as a Chartered Accountant in 2021, he continued at MSKA & Associates as an Assistant Manager until December 2024.

In January 2025, Jithesh S embarked on his independent professional journey as a freelance Chartered Accountant, offering services in bookkeeping, audit support, Internal Financial Controls (IFC) documentation, GST and income tax compliance, financial planning, and various statutory filings. As a trusted advisor to multiple clients, he brings a comprehensive and hands-on approach to solving complex financial and compliance challenges across industries.

With vast experience across diverse industries including manufacturing (beverages, aviation services), SaaS, biotechnology, and third-party administration (TPA) services. He is well-versed in Ind AS, IFRS and US GAAP, having contributed to IPO readiness, technical accounting memos, and high-impact audit deliverables.

He has also provided services across the deal continuum, though he has mainly focused on due diligence reviews, primarily in post-deal integrations, strategic portfolio reviews, initial public offerings, reorganizations and restructuring processes.
